Youtube Interests

Space Science stuffs

Here I will share my top channel choices where you can explore the beauty of universe and it's science.

kurzgesagt - in a Nutshell

This channel is a Treasure when it comes to simplicity and beautiful animation.
Here they explain most of the complex topics in a way even a kid can understand
and appreciate the mechanics of universe. They also explain about the possibities of
futuristic technologies and hypothetical scenarios with science. This channel is perfect
for those who want to learn about universe but donot want to go into the maths.

PBS Space Time

This channel is basically a more technical version of the previous one. Here you will
learn about the universe with some toppings of mathematics. The channel is not that
technical but it will help you understand how the universe works under the hood better.
Analogically this channel teaches c language instead of assembly language. you
know what I mean.

SEA

This channel contains high quality documentaries on the topics of space such
as Black Holes, Big Bang, Dark matter, Dark energy, you name it. This channel
is a daily dose for my sleep.

Engineering Stuffs

Here I will share my top channel choices which can make you fall in love with engineering.

Real Engineering

This channel focuses on the engineering of marvels that are being created or are
from past such as curiosity rover, perseverence, SR-71 Blackbird, x-15, 3D printing,
renewable energy amd many more. This channel is completely theoritical on explaining
stuff.

Hacksmith

This is one of my favourite channels. Here they pick technologies from movies, shows,
games, comics and try make them with present technologies such as Iron man
equipments, Captain America's shield, ligtsaber (every nerd's dream weapon). Its quite fun
to see futuristic techs coming to life using todays engineering.

ALex Lab

This channel quite similar to Hacksmith but more focused on creating iron man techs

Sci-Fi Stuffs

Here I will share my top Channel choices which have Sci-Fi terms in it.

Isaac Arthur

This channel focuses on exploring concepts in science with an emphasis on futurism
and space exploration, along with a healthy dose of science fiction. While we explore
many concepts and technologies that are far beyond us now, we try to keep everything
inside the bounds of known science or major theories.

Because Science

This channel uses real world science and maths to make sense of pop culture quandaries
in comics, video games, movies and shows
